The Situation

Bangladesh is one of the world's most densely populated countries, with a large percentage of its citizens living in poverty. It's known that insufficient access to basic necessities like food, clean water, and shelter continues to be a problem for millions of people worldwide, even in spite of notable advancements in economic growth and poverty reduction in recent years.

Many orphans and families are extremely vulnerable, facing daily hardships in finding the bare necessities to survive. The situation has intensified by frequent natural disasters, including floods and cyclones, causing families to be displaced and infrastructure to be destroyed.

Our goal is to provide relief to those most in need, offering support and resources to families and orphans who cannot support themselves.
